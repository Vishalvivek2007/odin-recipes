Fried Rice

A versatile dish made from stir-frying cooked rice in a wok or pan. It's commonly mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat, and seasoned with soy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Rice (cold, leftover rice works best)
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (e.g., Peas, Diced Carrots, Corn, Diced Onions)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cooking Oil
  • Protein (optional: Diced Chicken, Shrimp, or Tofu)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onions and other hard vegetables (like carrots) and stir-fry for a minute.
  3. Push vegetables to one side, pour beaten eggs onto the empty side, and scramble.
  4. Add the cold rice to the pan. Break it up with a spoon.
  5. Stir-fry the rice, mixing it with the eggs and vegetables.
  6. Add peas, corn, and any pre-cooked protein.
  7. Add soy sauce and mix until everything is evenly colored and hot.
