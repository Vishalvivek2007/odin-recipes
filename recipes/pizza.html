Pizza

pizza

A popular Italian dish consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients (toppings), which is then baked at a high temperature.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough (or pre-made base)
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Toppings (e.g., Pepperoni, Mushrooms, Onions, Bell Peppers)
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a flat surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking pan or pizza stone.
  4. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your desired toppings.
  7. Bake until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
