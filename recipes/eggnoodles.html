Egg Noodles

Egg noodles (spicy)

A quick and simple Asian stir-fry dish. It features boiled noodles tossed in a hot wok or pan with scrambled eggs, vegetables, and a savory sauce (usually soy-based).

Ingredients:

  • Noodles (e.g., Chow Mein or Ramen)
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (e.g., Cabbage, Carrots, Bean Sprouts)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cooking Oil
  • Onion & Garlic (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles according to package directions, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok.
  3. Scramble the eggs in the pan, then remove and set them aside.
  4. Add a bit more oil, then stir-fry the onion, garlic, and other vegetables until tender.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the vegetables.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce and add the scrambled eggs back in.
  7. Toss everything together until well-combined and heated through.
